Overall Aesthetic: High-Spirituality, Devotional Elegance, Professional Trust, Interactive Wellness.

1. The Comprehensive Palette
Base (Backgrounds):

Page Background: Deep Cosmic Purple gradient (#1A0D3E to #2E1460), exactly preserving the depth and texture of the original Diya image background.

Container/Card Backgrounds: A slightly lighter Deep Cosmic Purple (#3C1A78) for structured modules, providing subtle depth.

Primary Religious Accent:

Auspicious Saffron: (#FF9933 or #F97316) Use prominently for key actions and to denote Hindu spiritual context.

Devotional & Traditional Accent:

Devotional Gold: (#D4AF37 to #F2D06D) Use for structure, borders, and sub-headings to match the logo and Diya outline.

Interactive & Chakra Accent:

Chakra Glowing Blue: (#33D1FF) A clear, glowing blue referencing the logo’s heart chakra and the third eye, used for digital interactions.

Text & Contrast (Visibility is Key):

Pearl Cream (Light Cream): (#FFFDF7) A very light, warm cream used for primary, bold headings.

Lavender Light Grey: (#EAE3F7) A clean, legible light grey with a purple undertone for body text and descriptive paragraphs.

Deep Saffron: (#CC7A2A) For smaller text elements within Saffron containers, ensuring readability.

2. Detailed UI Element Application
Use these guidelines across all pages of the website to create a cohesive, professional look.

Headers & Footers
Header Bar: Solid Deep Cosmic Purple background, trimmed at the top edge with a 2-pixel precise Saffron border.

Logo: Place the full circular gold logo (image_7.png/image_10.png) on the left, looking sharp and high-resolution.

Navigation Links: Text is Pearl Cream for default state. Change to Chakra Glowing Blue on hover.

Footer Bar: Solid Deep Cosmic Purple background. Contact text in Lavender Light Grey. Footer text small labels in Devotional Gold. Social media icons (Gold and Saffron versions).

Typography
H1 (Primary Titles): Use a bold, elegant serif font in Pearl Cream text. (e.g., on Hero section)

H2 (Sub-headings): Same elegant serif font in Devotional Gold. (e.g., Section titles)

H3/H4/H5/H6 (Small Titles): Clean sans-serif font in Lavender Light Grey with a Saffron or Gold icon.

Body Text (p): Highly legible, clean sans-serif in Lavender Light Grey.

Buttons & Calls-to-Action (CTAs)
Primary CTA Button (e.g., "Join a Ramkatha"):

Background: Solid Auspicious Saffron.

Text: Bold Deep Cosmic Purple.

Hover State: Background shifts to a slightly darker Deep Saffron (#E67E22); Text remains dark purple. Focus ring is Chakra Glowing Blue.

Secondary/Donation Button (e.g., "Donate Now"):

Type: Gold Outline Button.

Border: Fine Devotional Gold line.

Text: Lavender Light Grey.

Hover State: Full background fill of Devotional Gold with Deep Purple text.

Small Icons/Utility Buttons: Use Gold by default, changing to Saffron on hover.

Form Elements
Input Fields/Textareas: Deep Cosmic Purple background, fine Gold border by default.

Active/Focus State: Border changes to a distinct Chakra Glowing Blue ring. Text is Pearl Cream.

Checkboxes/Radios: Checkmarks/Filled circles are Chakra Glowing Blue.

Interactive Highlights & State Changes
Use Chakra Glowing Blue as the primary digital feedback color for focus borders, progress bars, and active tab indicators.

Dividers & Structural Rules
Use fine, 1-pixel Devotional Gold lines as structural dividers (e.g., between menu items, below headings).
